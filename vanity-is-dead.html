The Premise
"This is not just an event. It is the content moment, the pipeline moment, and the credibility moment that anchors everything we do this year."
What we are saying

Social is not a flashy add-on any more. It shows up in the boardroom. Every panel, every keynote, every conversation at this event will drive home one message: integrated social delivers commercial outcomes, and the brands that understand that are winning.


We position Jungle Creations as the integrated marketing partner that connects brands to audiences and culture. Not a social agency. Not a content shop. A partner that sits at the strategic table.

Session themes
  • Panel one: from campaign to commercial. How a Jungle client turned social into a measurable growth driver. Real numbers on stage.
  • Keynote guest: why social sits in the boardroom now. A senior marketer from a brand we want to win speaking to peers, not to us.
  • Social maturity preview: what does good actually look like in 2026? A framework for brands that want to move beyond vanity.
  • Panel two: the integrated future. Platforms, brands, and partners on where social is going and who is built to take brands there.

Run of Show

The agenda.

1:30pm Doors Open Networking
Registration and networking. Light refreshments. Room set for conference.
2:00pm Opening Keynote Keynote — Jade
Jade sets the provocation. Vanity metrics are dead. What matters now is commercial impact and cultural connection. 15 minutes.
2:20pm Panel One Panel
Moderator, a Jungle client, and a creator. Real numbers, real outcomes. What did this campaign actually do for the business? 30 minutes.
2:55pm Keynote Guest Keynote — Guest
A senior marketer from a brand we want to win. Not a client yet. We give them the platform. 15–20 minutes on why social now sits inside boardroom conversations.
3:20pm Break Break — 20 min
Teas, coffees, croissants. This is where the real networking happens.
3:40pm Social Maturity Framework Preview
The team present an early preview of the social maturity framework. Attendees receive exclusive pre-access codes. Builds anticipation for the full 2027 launch.
4:10pm Panel Two Panel
Moderator, a platform partner, a brand we want to win, and a Jungle partnerships client. Where is social going and who is getting it right? 30 minutes.
4:45pm Close Keynote — Close
Jade or Mallory wraps. What we heard today, what we are building toward, what comes next. 10 minutes.
5:00pm Happy Hour 1hr Open Bar
One hour behind the bar. Music, merch, good drinks. This is where relationships are made.
6:00pm+ Targeted After Pipeline
Pre-planned dinners or drinks with priority brands. Coris and the team know who they are sitting with and why. This is where pipeline conversations happen.
