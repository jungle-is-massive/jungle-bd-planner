Jungle Brand Event — Oct 2026

The Social
Maturity Index

A Jungle Creations flagship event bringing together senior brand marketers to explore what separates high-performing social strategies from the rest. Attendees will leave with a clearer understanding of where their brand sits on the social maturity curve — and what moving up actually looks like.

Date1 Oct 2026 — TBC
FormatPanel + Roundtable, Invite-Only
VenueTBC — London
Target Attendance40–60 senior brand marketers
01

Event Definition

Event Name
The Social Maturity Index
Strapline
Where do brands really stand — and what does it take to move up?
What attendees will get
A benchmark for where their brand sits on the social maturity curve. Peer perspectives from senior marketers across sectors. Practical frameworks for making the case for social investment internally. Jungle's proprietary SMI data and methodology — first look.
Target audience
CMOs, Marketing Directors and Head of Social at brand-side organisations. Mix of existing Jungle clients, warm prospects, and new contacts introduced via intermediary relationships. Target sectors: FMCG, retail, finance, tech.
Topic / theme
Social Maturity — the gap between where most brands are operating and where they need to be. The conversation will centre on commercial performance, not vanity metrics. Jungle will introduce a framework for measuring maturity across five dimensions: strategy, content, distribution, data, and culture.
